About the Game

Logic with a visual twist

We took classic logic puzzles and gave them a colorful twist that changes everything.
Players rely on observation and deduction to safely clear tiles and avoid mistakes.
You'll need sharp eyes and a good memory for patterns as things get trickier.

How to Play

Look, think, then make your move

  • Analyze the Grid

    Take a good look at what's already showing and see how colors connect.

  • Interpret Clues

    Use color patterns to predict safe moves.

  • Clear Tiles

    Reveal sections of the board step by step.

  • Avoid Mistakes

    Incorrect decisions can block progress.

  • Complete the Board

    Clear all required tiles to finish the level.

Features

The gameplay's pretty straightforward - no unnecessary bells and whistles

  • Color-Based Logic

    Instead of dealing with numbers like in other logic games, you're working with colors.

  • Gradual Difficulty

    Puzzles become more complex over time.

  • Minimalist Design

    No clutter, no ads popping up - just you and the puzzle.

  • Replay Value

    Multiple boards to solve and master.

  • Smooth Interaction

    Controls actually do what you expect them to (which is rarer than it should be these days).

Game Modes

Different ways to play

  • Classic Mode

    Standard puzzles with increasing difficulty.

  • Timed Mode

    Solve the grid under time pressure.

  • Relax Mode

    Play without penalties or restrictions.
