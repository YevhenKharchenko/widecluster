About the Game

Same old match-3 you know and love,
just with way more cute factor

Look, if you've played Candy Crush, you'll get this immediately. The difference?
Instead of candy, you're matching stuff while cartoon pets bounce around being adorable.
Players move through levels by completing objectives while enjoying lively animations and rewarding feedback.
Super easy to learn, and honestly? I found myself playing way longer than I planned to.
